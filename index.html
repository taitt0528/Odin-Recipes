Specialty Banh Mi:

Ingredients:

  • Cilantro
  • Vietnamese Red Pork Jambon (Thit Do)
  • FlowerÂ© Pate
  • Vietnamese Mayonnaise
  • French Sesame Baguette
  • Cucumbers
  • Green Chili Peppers
  • Head cheese (Not actually cheese)
  • Vietnamese Pickled Daikon and Carrots

Instructions:

Ingredient Preparation

  1. Reheat Baguette
  2. Cut open Baguette
  3. Thinly slice your head cheese
  4. Thinly slice your Vietnamese pork ham
  5. Horizontally slice your cucumbers
  6. Slice green chili into circles

Assembly

  1. Spread Vietnamese mayo onto both sides of the baguette
  2. Open Pate Can
  3. Spread Pate onto both sides of Baguette
  4. Add the folded slices of head cheese and Vietnamese ham, alternating between each type
  5. Add Pickled Veggies on top of sliced meats
  6. Add Cilantro on top of Pickled Veggies
  7. Add Cucumber on top of Pickled Veggies
  8. Optional Add Sliced Chili on top of Cucumbers
